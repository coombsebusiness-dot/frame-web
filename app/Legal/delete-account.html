Delete Your I'm Sorted Account

This page explains how to request deletion of your I'm Sorted account and associated app data.

How to delete your account

You can delete your account directly inside the I'm Sorted app.

  1. Open the I'm Sorted app.
  2. Go to your account or settings screen.
  3. Select Delete Account.
  4. Confirm that you want to permanently delete your account.

Once confirmed, your account deletion request will be processed.

What data is deleted?

When your account is deleted, we delete personal data associated with your account, including:

  • Your account profile information
  • Saved documents
  • Uploaded document images or files
  • Extracted text from documents
  • AI-generated document summaries
  • Detected appointments and action items

What may be retained?

Some limited information may be retained where required for legal, security, fraud prevention, accounting, or compliance purposes.

Any retained information will only be kept for as long as necessary.

Need help?

If you cannot access the app or need help deleting your account, contact us at:

help@imsorted.co.uk

Request Account Deletion

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.
